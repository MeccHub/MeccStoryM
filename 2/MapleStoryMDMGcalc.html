Calculation Formulas

base = ATK × (SkillDMG/100) × (1 + DMG%/100) × (1 + FinalDMG%/100 + SkillFinalDMG%/100) × (1 + ATK%/100)
nonCritTotal = base × HitCount
critTotal = base × (1.2 + CritDMG%/100) × HitCount
avgTotal = (1 - CritRate%/100) × nonCritTotal + (CritRate%/100) × critTotal

bossBase = ATK × (SkillDMG/100) × (1 + DMG%/100) × (1 + FinalDMG%/100 + SkillFinalDMG%/100) × (1 + ATK%/100 + (SkillDMG/100 × BossATK%/100))
bossNonCritTotal = bossBase × HitCount
bossCritTotal = bossBase × (1.2 + CritDMG%/100) × HitCount
bossAvgTotal = (1 - CritRate%/100) × bossNonCritTotal + (CritRate%/100) × bossCritTotal

Per-line values = Total ÷ HitCount
